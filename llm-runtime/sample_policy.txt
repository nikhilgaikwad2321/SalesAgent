TERM INSURANCE POLICY - GOLD PLAN

1. OVERVIEW
The Gold Plan Term Insurance provides comprehensive life cover with affordable premiums.
The policy ensures financial security for your family in your absence.

2. KEY FEATURES
- Sum Assured: Up to $1 Million
- Policy Term: 10 to 40 years
- Entry Age: 18 to 65 years
- Premium Payment: Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually

3. EXCLUSIONS
- Suicide within the first 12 months of policy inception.
- Death due to participation in hazardous sports.

4. CLAIM PROCESS
- Intimate the claim within 24 hours via our portal.
- Submit required documents (Death Certificate, Policy Document, ID Proof).
- Claims are settled within 7 working days.

5. IMPORTANT NOTE
- This policy does NOT offer guaranteed returns. It is a pure risk cover.
- Premiums are subject to market tax rates.
